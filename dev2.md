## Core Student Experience

### 1. Consistent AI Highlighting

* Identify only the most important sentences or formulas.
* Limit highlights per page.
* Prevent duplicate and overlapping highlights.
* Cache highlights so they remain consistent across sessions.

### 2. Highlight-Based Explanations

When the user hovers over a highlight, show a small contextual popover on the right tab. Otherwise it's empty probably. 

For normal text, it can include:

* Why this passage matters
* A simpler explanation
* Its connection to the surrounding section

Keep the explanation short so it does not interrupt reading.

### 3. Dark Mode

* Improve contrast between the document, highlights, and interface.
* Use softer near-black backgrounds.
* Ensure highlights remain readable without appearing overly bright.
* Refine the pet animation, popovers, buttons, and hover states for dark mode.

### 4. Automatic Math Explanations

When Axiom highlights a formula, hovering over it should show:

* A natural-language explanation
* Definitions of important symbols
* Pseudocode when it meaningfully clarifies the formula

This should happen through the same highlight interaction rather than requiring a separate button.

## Core Interaction

**Axiom highlights something important → the user hovers → Axiom explains why it matters.**
